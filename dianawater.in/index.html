OUR PROCESS

From Source to Every Sip

Every bottle follows a carefully managed journey, where every stage is designed to preserve freshness, maintain consistency and deliver a refreshing drinking experience.

Water Source

Carefully sourced water enters our purification system.

RO Filtration

Reverse Osmosis removes impurities and dissolved contaminants.

UV Treatment

Ultraviolet purification helps eliminate microorganisms.

Ozonization

Ozone treatment maintains freshness and product safety.

Mineral Balance
